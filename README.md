# 📦 E-Commerce Operations & Customer Intelligence System

## 📌 Project Overview

The E-Commerce Operations & Customer Intelligence System is a comprehensive data analytics project designed to analyze supply chain operations, customer behavior, sales performance, and logistics efficiency using Python, MySQL, SQL, and Power BI.

The project transforms raw supply chain data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, advanced SQL analytics, and interactive Power BI dashboards. The objective is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying revenue opportunities, customer trends, operational bottlenecks, and profitability risks.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze sales and revenue performance across product categories and regions.
* Identify high-value customers and customer segments.
* Evaluate supply chain and shipping performance.
* Detect loss-making products and profitability issues.
* Generate strategic recommendations to improve business performance.
* Develop executive-level dashboards for decision-making.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### Revenue Insights

* Sporting Goods generated the highest sales revenue.
* Western Europe contributed the largest share of overall revenue.

### Customer Insights

* Consumer customers generated the majority of total sales.
* High-value customers contributed a significant portion of overall revenue.

### Supply Chain Insights

* Second Class Shipping experienced the highest average delivery delays.
* Certain products generated negative profit margins despite strong sales performance.

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

1. Focus marketing investments on the Consumer segment to maximize revenue growth.

3. Review pricing and discount strategies for loss-making products.
4. Expand successful sales strategies from high-performing regions into emerging markets.
5. Implement customer loyalty and retention programs for high-value customers.
